    Our Vision

    Operation Revive aims to bring mobile, affordable cancer diagnosis to 25 million people in slums.

    The company's program is multi-tiered, and it is designed to overcome many of the barriers slum dwellers face regarding healthcare. Revive's plan is to conduct early cancer diagnosis, thereby greatly reducing treatment costs and increasing the chance for recovery and survival.

    Our goal is to partner with local organizations to strengthen the reach into the community and to develop programs tailored for individual communities. By working with the existing network of providers and organizations, we can enhance and strengthen available resources.

    Additionally, core parts of the program involve educational outreach and employment, both of which will empower individuals to improve their own communities.

    Revive was founded in the fall of 2013 by five Hult International Business School MBA students from North America, India, Europe and Asia. The team brings together professional backgrounds in engineering, marketing and communications, medical devices and entrepreneurship.
